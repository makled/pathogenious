  1. Your Notepad

    Here you will find all the notes of things you know so far regarding the case.

  2. XP and Rank

    XP: Experiences you gained helping out in this scenario.

    Rank: Your rank among other players.

  3. Scenarios Solved

    This shows you the number of scenarios you have solved in each category.

  4. Action Button

    This is your Action Button. With this button you could move from room to room or eliminate choices while playing.

  5. Your Profile

    This is where you can go to your profile and check out your progress in each category.

  6. Scenarios and Choices

    Here you will go to different rooms and see different cases. You will be asked to give your input through choices that will be given to you.

  7. Tour Has Ended

    You could always come back to this tour whenever you feel like you need to remember something.
